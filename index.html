VirtuaMonster

An incremental game where you raise and evolve your monster.

Created using Apache Cordova.

Apache Cordova is a platform that allows developers to create multi-platform and native applications using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. This means that VirtuaMonster can be enjoyed on almost every mobile device.

Power-Up Your Monster! Spend ability points as your monster grows.

As your monster grows, you gain points. Use these points to buy meteors that will increase your monster's growth rate. Buy carefully though, because what meteors you choose and how many of each you have will determine what your monster will evolve into.

Customize with Items! Equipt Style Sheets to your monster to change its appearence.

As you raise your monster you will recieve currency for special events like leveling your monster up or evolveing it. Use this currency for items to take care of your monster such as food and bandages, but also use it to buy style sheets which will alow you to customize your monster.
